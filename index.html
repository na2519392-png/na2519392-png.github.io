How many days does it take for a post to get approved?Post approve hone mein kitne din lagte hain?
A post is reviewed within a minimum of 7 days after submission. Once approved, your reward is credited based on approval. Minimum withdrawal: $20. Post submit karne ke baad minimum 7 din mein review ho jati hai. Approve hone ke baad reward based on approval milta hai. Minimum withdrawal: $20.
Do I need to add views to my video?Video par views lagane ki zaroorat hai?
You can use content promotion tools to improve reach organically, but organic engagement is encouraged. Views are not required for post approval — they are optional. Even with zero views, your post can be fully approved and qualify for a reward based on approval. Aap content promotion tools use kar sakte hain reach behtar karne ke liye, lekin organic engagement ko prefer kiya jata hai. Views post approve hone ke liye zarori nahi — yeh optional hai. Zero views ke bawajood post approve ho sakti hai aur reward based on approval mil sakta hai.
Why does it say "Disclose" or "Ad Content" on my post?Post par "Disclose" ya "Ad Content" kyun likha aata hai?
This happens because when we copy the TikTok link and authorize it with the "Ads" option enabled, we are giving Temu permission to run ads on our video. Temu then sponsors our video after approval.

According to TikTok's rules, once authorized for ads — you cannot delete that video. This has no negative effect on your account. The "Disclose" label simply means your video is an authorized ad — that's a normal part of the program. Yeh isliye hota hai kyunki jab hum TikTok link copy karte hain aur "Ads" option on karke authorize karte hain — Temu ko apni video par ad chalane ki permission milti hai. Approve hone ke baad Temu hamaari video sponsor karta hai.

TikTok ka rule hai ke authorize hone ke baad woh video delete nahi ho sakti. Iska koi negative effect nahi hai. "Disclose" sirf yeh batata hai ke yeh authorized ad hai — yeh program ka ek normal hissa hai.
How many videos can I upload per day?Din mein kitni videos upload kar sakte hain?
You can upload as many as you want — 5, 10, or more per day. There is no limit. More posts = more submission chances = better activity outcome. Jitni chaaho utni — 5, 10 ya isse bhi zyada din mein. Koi limit nahi. Zyada posts = zyada submission chances = behtar activity outcome.
What is the minimum amount I can withdraw?Minimum kitne dollar nikal sakte hain?
The minimum withdrawal amount is $20. Once your post is approved and reward is credited based on approval, withdraw when your balance reaches $20 or more. Minimum withdrawal amount $20 hai. Post approve hone aur reward based on approval milne ke baad jab balance $20 ya zyada ho jaye tab withdraw kar sakte hain.
How do I maximize my program results?Program results zyada kaise karein?
Authorize your account with the highest follower count. More followers = higher level = better activity outcome per post. Example: 10K followers → ~$5/post, 100K → ~$60/post, 1M → $100+/post. Results vary depending on campaign.
Always authorize the account that has the most followers — this directly determines your program level. Actual rewards depend on performance and platform approval.
 Sabse zyada followers wale account se authorize karo. Zyada followers = zyada level = behtar activity outcome. Example: 10K followers → ~$5/post, 1 lakh → ~$60/post, 10 lakh → $100+/post. Results campaign ke mutabiq alag hote hain.

Is Temu a trusted platform? Why does it offer rewards?Kya Temu trusted hai? Rewards kyun deta hai?
Yes — Temu is a globally recognized e-commerce platform, similar to Amazon and Shopify. Creators who participate in approved campaigns may receive rewards based on approval.

Why rewards? If Temu creates its own ad campaign, it costs significantly more. By working with creators through this program, they promote their products through real accounts. Both sides can benefit — though actual results vary per individual and campaign. Haan — Temu ek globally recognized e-commerce platform hai, bilkul Amazon aur Shopify ki tarah. Jo creators approved campaigns mein participate karte hain, unhe based on approval rewards mil sakte hain.

Rewards kyun? Agar Temu khud ad campaign banaye toh zyada cost aati hai. Is program ke zariye creators ke accounts se promotion hoti hai. Dono ko faida ho sakta hai — lekin actual results individual aur campaign ke hisab se alag hote hain.

Monetized ho, demonetized ho, ya kabhi nahi hua — koi farq nahi. Sirf active account + 1,000+ followers chahiye.
